Log Files

Gloomy Media adheres to standard log file procedures. These files record visitor activities on our website, including internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISPs), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These log files do not contain personally identifiable information. We use this data for trend analysis, site administration, user tracking, and demographic information gathering.

CCPA Privacy Rights

Requesting the deletion of personal data collected about them.
Opting out of the sale of personal data.
We have one month to respond to your requests. To exercise these rights, please contact us.
